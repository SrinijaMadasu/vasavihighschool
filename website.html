Vasavi High School has different labs like Electronics Lab, Computer Lab, Instrumentation lab etc. Medical Medical Facility is provided by the School as the first-aid kit.

The school offer its students a perfect mix of academic, co-curricular and extra curricular activities necessary for all round development of a student.

Vasavi High School was established in August 1983 and has completed 35 years in imparting quality education. The school equips the children with knowledge and right values thus grooming the children in totality.
